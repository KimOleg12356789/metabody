Gallery

Frank - Pilot

Frank Cooper was a test pilot until he suffered a severe cerebellar injury. After 2 years of rehabilitation, he was still barely able to lift his hand with a glass of water. But scientists from the Institute of augmentation offered him to volunteer in a scientific experiment. Cranial implants not only restored the functioning of the brain, but also endowed Frank with extraordinary abilities. Perfect spatial orientation, 100% peripheral vision, improved coordination and agility. All this makes Mr. Cooper the perfect pilot.

Aneli — navigator

Reckless and impulsive - first she does, and then she thinks. But thanks to her determination, the crew more than once escaped from unexpected collisions, be it a meteorite or intergalactic criminals. Passed on the rights of category α-Centauri from the fifth time because of the explosive nature. She despises boredom, so she is always up to cause some chaos for fun.

Sasha — engineer

Don't be misled by her apearence. Sasha knows how every mechanism in the galaxy works. So busy with development that even Elon Musk can hardly get her personal advice on his next project. An absolute layman in everyday matters, so the ship has all the possible types of gadgets invented by Sasha, from a blanket tucker in a sleep capsule to an android cook who obeys the power of the owner’s thought. She spends all his free time on self-improvement, yoga and meditation.

Coco - Geneticist

She implanted herself with a super learning gene and now an iQ of 300 points is not the limit. Coco believes that there is an opportunity to solve the issue of time, so she is waiting for the evening when personal participation in the Darwin scientific survey is possible. Kind and caring, she will always support any crew member and find an individual approach to them.Very organized, but overly concerned with order and cleanliness.
